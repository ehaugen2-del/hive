Jessica Pring
Owner & Stylist



Hi I’m Jessica Pring the founder and a stylist at The Hive salon, I moved to the mountain about 10 years ago and have been in the industry for over 15 years. I specialize in blondes, color corrections, hand tied weft extensions, and full body waxing and I love helping each client achieve the look that makes them feel amazing. Whether you’re here for a fresh color, a transformative cut, or simply a moment of self-care The Hive is a place where you’ll always be seen, heard, and valued. I am passionate about helping my clients embrace their individuality and leave feeling renewed inside and out.
